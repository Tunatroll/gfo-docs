    ## River Intel
    
    River Intel is a concept for a data-driven Android application designed for fly fishers. It aims to leverage the power of local AI, running directly on the device, to analyze various river data points.
    
    ### Key Features:
    
    * **Real-time Data:** Integrates with available real-time river flow, temperature, and weather data sources.
    * **Local AI Analysis:** Utilizes on-device AI to process data, predict insect hatches, fish activity, and suggest optimal fishing times and locations based on historical and current conditions.
    * **User Input:** Allows users to log catches, observe conditions, and contribute to a community dataset, further refining the AI model locally.
    * **Offline Capability:** Local AI ensures core features work even without an internet connection, crucial for remote river locations.
    
    This approach ensures privacy and functionality regardless of connectivity, providing fly fishers with actionable insights powered by edge AI.
