    ## JVM Reverse Engineering
    Specializing in deep-system analysis of Java-based infrastructure. Our research focuses on:
    * **Bytecode Manipulation:** Analyzing complex JVM internals to identify logic flaws.
    * **Infrastructure Audits:** Decompiling and auditing mission-critical backend systems.
    * **Legacy Systems:** Securing and modernizing older Java environments.
    
    ## AI Safety & Agentic Vulnerabilities
    With the rise of Agentic AI, security must evolve. We leverage our local **RTX 5090** environment to perform:
    * **Prompt Injection Testing:** Identifying vulnerabilities in LLM-driven workflows.
    * **Data Exfiltration Research:** Ensuring local agents remain air-gapped and secure.
    * **Safety Benchmarking:** Stress-testing models like Gemma and Llama 3 for enterprise guardrails.
    
    ## Active Programs
    We participate in high-stakes bug bounty programs (e.g., Bugcrowd, Immunefi) focusing on critical infrastructure and AI safety.
